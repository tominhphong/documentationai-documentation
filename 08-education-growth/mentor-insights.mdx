## Best Practices Từ Top Agents

### Prospecting

| Practice | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Contact 5 people/day minimum | Industry standard |
| Follow up within 5 minutes of new lead | Speed-to-lead research |
| 80% of sales happen after 5th follow-up | NAR statistics |
| Open houses generate buyer leads | Experienced agents |

### Client Service

| Practice | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Update client weekly (even if no news) | Top producer habit |
| Under-promise, over-deliver | Universal advice |
| Know the answer or know who does | Mentors |
| Always be early to appointments | Professional standard |
