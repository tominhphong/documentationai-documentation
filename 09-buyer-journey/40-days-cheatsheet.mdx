## Documents Checklist — Chuẩn bị từ Day 1

**Income docs**
- [ ] W2 forms 2 năm (nếu employee)
- [ ] Pay stubs 30 ngày gần nhất
- [ ] 1099 forms 2 năm (nếu self-employed)
- [ ] Tax returns 2 năm (bao gồm Schedule C cho 1099)
- [ ] Profit/Loss statement YTD (nếu business owner)

**Asset docs**
- [ ] Bank statements 2 tháng (all accounts, all pages)
- [ ] Investment account statements (401K, IRA, brokerage)
- [ ] Gift letter + donor bank statement (nếu gift money)

**Identity docs**
- [ ] Government ID (driver license hoặc passport)
- [ ] Social Security card
- [ ] Green card (nếu permanent resident) hoặc work visa docs

**During transaction**
- [ ] BMA-1 ký (Stage 2)
- [ ] Executed contract (Stage 6)
- [ ] Inspection report (Stage 7)
- [ ] Amendment/repair request (Stage 7)
- [ ] Appraisal report (Stage 8)
- [ ] Homeowners insurance policy (Stage 8)
- [ ] Closing Disclosure (Stage 8, 3 ngày trước close)

## Numbers to Know — Chi phí điển hình

| Item | Typical Cost | Khi nào trả |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| Earnest money | 1-2% of price | Tại contract signed |
| Option fee | $100-300 | Tại contract signed |
| Inspection | $400-600 | Day 23-25 |
| Specialist inspections | $200-400/each | Optional, Day 23-25 |
| Appraisal | $500-700 | Day 25-28 (often pay at close) |
| Homeowners insurance (1 year prepaid) | $1,500-3,000 | Paid at close |
| Property tax (escrow prepaid) | Varies by county | Paid at close |
| Title insurance | ~0.9% of price | Paid at close |
| Loan origination | 0.5-1% of loan | Paid at close |
| **Total closing cost (buyer)** | **2-4% of price** | — |
| Down payment | 3% (FHA) – 20% (conventional) | Wired day of close |

**Example — $400K home:**
- Earnest money: $4,000-8,000
- Down payment 10%: $40,000
- Closing cost 3%: ~$12,000
- Total cash needed: ~$56,000

## Texas-specific Milestones

- **Option period:** 7-10 ngày standard. Terminate với earnest money back có quyền, sau period chỉ back out được với contingency failure.
- **Homestead exemption:** Deadline April 30 năm sau close. Save ~$500-800/năm property tax.
- **Wire fraud:** Texas is #1 state cho real estate wire fraud. **ALWAYS verify wire instructions bằng phone call** trực tiếp tới title company.
- **PID/MUD districts:** Common ở Sherman/Frisco new construction. Thêm ~0.5-1.5% property tax. Check [Sherman PID/MUD Taxes](../03-market-intelligence/sherman-pid-mud-taxes).

## Red Flags — Phải Alert Ngay

- Inspection phát hiện foundation cracks ngang (structural)
- Inspection phát hiện roof age >20 năm + leak signs
- Appraisal thấp hơn contract price >5%
- Seller từ chối ký amendment cho safety items (GFCI, smoke detector)
- Title search phát hiện lien hoặc claim chưa clear
- HOA docs reveal special assessment pending >$5K
- Flood zone disclosure không mention trong listing

Gọi Phong ngay nếu gặp bất kỳ item nào.
