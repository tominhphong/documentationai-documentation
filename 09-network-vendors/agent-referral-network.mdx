## Referral Fee Agreement

| | Standard |
|--|---------|
| **Fee** | 25% of gross commission |
| **Paid by** | Receiving agent's brokerage |
| **Form** | eXp referral agreement |
| **When paid** | At closing |

## Script Gửi Referral

> "Hi [Agent Name], I have a client relocating to [city]. They're looking for [type of home/price range]. Would you be able to help them? If so, I'll send over a referral agreement. My client speaks [Vietnamese/English]. Please let me know!"

## Script Xin Referral Từ Agent Khác

> "Hi [Agent Name], I specialize in the Sherman/Grayson County area of North Texas. If you ever have clients looking in this area, I'd love to help! I'm fluent in Vietnamese and English, and I focus on the Vietnamese community. Happy to send referral fee for any clients you send my way."
