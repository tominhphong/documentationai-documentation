# Post-Closing Survey SOP

<Callout kind="info">
  Survey gửi 2 tuần sau closing — đủ thời gian để khách settle in nhưng experience còn fresh. Dùng Deftform (LTD) để tạo survey.
</Callout>

## Timeline

| Thời điểm | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Closing day | Congratulations + gift |
| 1 tuần sau | Check-in call/text |
| **2 tuần sau** | **Gửi survey** |
| 3 tuần sau | Google Review request (nếu survey positive) |

## 5 Câu Hỏi Survey

| # | Câu hỏi | Type |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | Overall, how was your experience working with Phong? | 1-5 stars |
| 2 | How would you rate communication throughout the process? | 1-5 stars |
| 3 | Did Phong demonstrate strong knowledge of the market? | 1-5 stars |
| 4 | Would you recommend Phong to friends and family? | Yes/No/Maybe |
| 5 | Any suggestions for how Phong can improve? | Open text |

### Vietnamese

> 
> Em muốn xin anh/chị vài phút để chia sẻ feedback — giúp em cải thiện dịch vụ cho khách hàng sau. Chỉ 5 câu hỏi ngắn thôi ạ:
> 
> [Link survey]
> 
> Cảm ơn anh/chị nhiều!"

### English

> "Hi [Name], I hope you're enjoying your new home! 🏡
> 
> I'd love to hear about your experience. Would you mind taking 2 minutes to share your feedback? It helps me improve for future clients:
> 
> [Link survey]
> 
> Thank you so much!"

## Xử Lý Feedback

### Positive (4-5 stars + Would recommend)

1. Thank them immediately
2. Ask for Google Review (xem Google Review Requests)
3. Ask permission to use as testimonial
4. Add to testimonials database

### Neutral (3 stars)

1. Thank them for honest feedback
2. Address specific concerns
3. Ask what could have been better
4. Implement improvements

### Negative (1-2 stars)

1. Thank them — DO NOT get defensive
2. Call (don't text) to discuss
3. Apologize for shortcomings
4. Create action plan to improve
5. Follow up in 1 month
6. **DO NOT** ask for public review
