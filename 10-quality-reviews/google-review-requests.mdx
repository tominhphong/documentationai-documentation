## Respond to Reviews

### Positive Review Response

> "Thank you so much [Name]! It was a pleasure working with you and [spouse if applicable]. Wishing you all the best in your new home! If you ever need anything, I'm just a call away. 🏡"

### Negative Review Response

> "Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. I'm sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. I take every concern seriously and would love the opportunity to discuss this further. Please feel free to call me at (737) 242-9999. Your satisfaction matters greatly to me."

**Rules for responding:**
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48 hours
- Keep it professional and warm
- Don't argue publicly
- Take negative discussions offline (phone)
- Thank even critical reviewers
