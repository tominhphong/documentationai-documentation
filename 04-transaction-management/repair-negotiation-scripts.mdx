# Repair Negotiation Scripts

<Callout kind="info">
  Repair negotiation là nghệ thuật — không phải khoa học. Mục tiêu: bảo vệ quyền lợi khách hàng nhưng giữ deal sống. Phong sẽ customize scripts theo từng tình huống cụ thể.
</Callout>

## Nguyên Tắc Chung

| Nguyên tắc | Chi tiết |
|------------|---------|
| Focus on safety & structural | Đừng yêu cầu repair cosmetic items |
| Provide estimates | Gửi kèm estimate từ contractor nếu có |
| Offer alternatives | Repair HOẶC Credit — cho seller lựa chọn |
| Stay professional | Không attack seller, focus on facts |
| Know market conditions | Seller's market → ít leverage. Buyer's market → nhiều leverage |

## Script 1: Yêu Cầu Repair Từ Seller

### Template Chung

> "Based on the home inspection report dated [date], Buyer requests the following repairs be completed by licensed, insured contractors prior to closing:
> 
> 1. **[Hạng mục]** — [Mô tả vấn đề từ inspection report]
>    - Estimated repair cost: $[___]
> 
> 2. **[Hạng mục]** — [Mô tả vấn đề]
>    - Estimated repair cost: $[___]
> 
> Total estimated cost: $[___]
> 
> Buyer is willing to accept completion of repairs OR a closing cost credit of $[___] in lieu of repairs."

### Khi Có Foundation Issue

> "The inspection report identified foundation concerns including [specific issues — cracks, settling, drainage]. This is a structural and safety issue that significantly impacts the property's value and livability.
> 
> Buyer requests:
> - Option A: Seller to hire a licensed foundation repair company to assess and repair, with transferable warranty, prior to closing
> - Option B: Seller credit of $[estimate] at closing for Buyer to address post-closing
> 
> Buyer also requests a re-inspection after repairs are completed."

### Khi Có Roof Issue

> "The inspection report indicates the roof [specific issue — age, missing shingles, leaks, damaged flashing]. Given that roof replacement/repair is a significant expense ($[estimate]), Buyer requests:
> 
> - Option A: Seller to replace/repair roof by a licensed roofing contractor with warranty, prior to closing
> - Option B: Seller credit of $[estimate] at closing
> 
> Please advise which option Seller prefers."

### Khi Có HVAC Issue

> "The HVAC system [specific issue — age exceeds expected lifespan, not cooling/heating properly, unusual noises]. Buyer's inspector noted the unit is [age] years old (typical lifespan is 15-20 years).
> 
> Buyer requests:
> - Option A: Seller to have HVAC serviced/repaired/replaced by a licensed HVAC contractor prior to closing
> - Option B: Seller credit of $[estimate] at closing
> - Option C: Seller to provide a Home Warranty covering HVAC for the first year"

### Khi Có Plumbing Issue

> "The inspection report identified plumbing concerns: [specific — leaks, old galvanized pipes, water heater age, sewer line issues]. Buyer requests Seller to address these items by a licensed plumber prior to closing, OR provide a credit of $[estimate] at closing."

## Script 2: Từ Chối Repair Nhưng Giữ Deal

### Khi Đại Diện Seller — Từ Chối Toàn Bộ

> "Thank you for the repair request. After reviewing the inspection report, Seller respectfully declines the requested repairs. The property was listed and priced in as-is condition, and Seller believes the current contract price reflects the property's condition.
> 
> Seller remains committed to closing this transaction and is open to discussing other solutions that work for both parties."

### Khi Đại Diện Seller — Từ Chối Một Phần

> "Thank you for the repair request. Seller has reviewed each item and responds as follows:
> 
> - **[Hạng mục 1]** — AGREE to repair prior to closing
> - **[Hạng mục 2]** — DECLINE — this is a cosmetic/maintenance item, not a defect
> - **[Hạng mục 3]** — Counter with credit of $[___] instead of repair
> 
> Seller believes this is a fair resolution that addresses the significant items while keeping the transaction moving forward."

### Khi Đại Diện Buyer — Seller Từ Chối

> "Anh/chị, seller đã từ chối yêu cầu repair. Mình có mấy options:
> 1. Chấp nhận as-is và tiến hành closing (nếu vấn đề không quá lớn)
> 2. Counter — giảm bớt yêu cầu, focus vào items quan trọng nhất
> 3. Yêu cầu credit thay vì repair (seller thường prefer option này)
> 4. Walk away trong Option Period (nếu vấn đề quá nghiêm trọng)
> 
> Mình recommend [option] vì [lý do]. Anh/chị muốn làm gì?"

## Script 3: Credit Thay Vì Repair

### Yêu Cầu Credit

> "In lieu of the requested repairs, Buyer is willing to accept a closing cost credit of $[total amount] to address the following items post-closing:
> 
> | Item | Estimated Cost |
> |------|---------------|
> | [Hạng mục 1] | $[___] |
> | [Hạng mục 2] | $[___] |
> | [Hạng mục 3] | $[___] |
> | **Total Credit** | **$[___]** |
> 
> This allows Seller to avoid coordinating repairs and provides Buyer the flexibility to address items on their own timeline after closing."

### Giải Thích Credit Cho Khách Người Việt

> "Anh/chị, thay vì yêu cầu seller sửa, mình có thể yêu cầu credit — tức là seller giảm tiền tại closing. Ví dụ: nếu cần sửa mái nhà ước tính $5,000, seller credit $5,000. Tiền này sẽ được trừ vào closing costs hoặc giảm giá nhà.
> 
> Lợi ích: Anh/chị tự chọn contractor, tự kiểm soát chất lượng, và làm theo timeline của mình. Seller cũng prefer option này vì không phải coordinate repairs."

## Bảng Tham Khảo Chi Phí Sửa Chữa

| Hạng mục | Chi phí ước tính | Ghi chú |
|----------|------------------|---------|
| Foundation repair (pier) | $500-$1,500/pier | Tùy số lượng piers |
| Roof replacement | $8,000-$15,000 | Tùy diện tích và vật liệu |
| HVAC replacement | $5,000-$10,000 | Tùy kích thước nhà |
| Water heater | $1,000-$2,500 | Tank vs tankless |
| Plumbing repair | $200-$2,000 | Tùy scope |
| Electrical panel upgrade | $1,500-$3,000 | 100 to 200 amp |
| Sewer line repair | $2,000-$5,000 | Tùy phương pháp |
| Termite treatment | $500-$1,500 | Tùy mức độ |
| Mold remediation | $1,000-$10,000 | Tùy diện tích |
